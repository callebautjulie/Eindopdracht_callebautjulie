Quadriofobia

Do you sometimes feel like you can't face the colors Cyan, Magenta, Yellow and Key? This fobia describes this feeling. You start to feel very sick, dizzy. It's just like you're unable to breath. There are a whole lot of people experiencing exactly the same problems.

Do you already know what this anxiety is? Be quick and become a part of the family. By funding this fobia, you'll give the people suffering from this, a change to get better.

Julie Callebaut, Arteveldehogeschool 2018
